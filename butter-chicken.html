Butter Chicken Recipe

Description

As much as I love making Indian food at home, sometimes I find the list of ingredients daunting. It’s easy enough to make once I gather all the ingredients, but that’s where I usually fall short because it requires a trip to an Indian grocery store – and my closest one is a fair drive away.

Ingredients

  • 0.5 cup plain yoghurt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp tumeric powder
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 0.5 tsp chilli powder
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ginger
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 750g chicken thigh fillets
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup tomato passata
  • 1 cup thickened cream
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1.25 tsp salt

Steps

  1. Marinade ingredients with chicken for 24 hours prior to cooking
  2. Heat the butter over high heat in large fry pan. Take chicken out of marinade but do not wipe away excess marinade.
  3. Place chicken in fry pan and cook for roughly 3 minutes until chicken is white
  4. Add tomato passata, cream, sugar and salt. Also add any remaining marinade left in bowl. Turn down low and simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Garnish with coriander and serve with basmati rice.
