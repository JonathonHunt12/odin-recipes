Pad Thai Recipe

Description

A beautiful and wholesome pad thai, complete with fresh pacific prawns, homegrown bean sprouts and juicy lemon. For those that want a taste of southeast Asia, this is for you.

This dish is easy to cook, only taking 20 minutes to prep and 10 minutes to cook. Make sure the noodles are soaked prior to cooking and that vegetables are fresh.

Ingredients

  • 125g of Chang's Pad Thai dried noodles
  • 3 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1.5 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1/2 onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 150g chicken breast
  • 2 eggs
  • 1.5 cups of bean sprouts
  • 0.5 cup of firm tofu
  • 0.25 cup of garlic chives
  • 0.25 cup finely chopped peanuts

Steps

  1. Place noodles in a large bowl, pour over plenty of boiling water. Soak for 5 minutes, then drain in a colander and rinse under cold water.
  2. Mix sauce in a bowl and make sure noodles aren't sitting for more than 10 minutes.
  3. Heat 2 tbsp of oil in a pan over high heat. Add garlic and onion for 30 seconds.
  4. Add and cook chicken mostly through for 1.5 minutes.
  5. Add egg to one side of the pan and scramble.
  6. Add bean sprouts, tofu, noodles then sauce.
  7. Toss for about 2 minutes until sauce is absorbed and then add the garlic and peanuts.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy!
