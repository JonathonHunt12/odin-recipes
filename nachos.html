Nachos Recipe

Description

Here we have it, the fan favourite. Nachos, in all their glory, tasty, spicy, flavourful and breaming with options. This nachos recipe will have you telling everyone you can about it. Always wanting more. This is authentic Mexican food!

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp chili powder
  • 1.5 tsp salt
  • 1 teaspon granulated onion
  • 1 teaspoon granulated garlic
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 0.5 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 0.25 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
  • 1 pound beef
  • 16 ounces refried beans
  • 0.25 cup of water
  • tortilla chips
  • 4 ounces cheddar cheese
  • 4 ounces Colby Jack cheese
  • 1 cup of pico de gallo
  • 1 jalepeno

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Combine all spices in a mix bowl
  3. Heat vegetable oil on medium high heat, add ground beef, spices. After 8 minutes drain the fat, add refried beans and water and reduce heat to low.
  4. Toast the chips in the preheated oven for 5 minutes
  5. Assemble the nachos on a tray, sprinkle the cheese all over the nachos and place in the oven to bake
  6. Top and serve with whatever takes your fancy.
